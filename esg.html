Lighthouse Guardian Program

Light minors’ way and guard their future.

ELFBAR always insists on caring about teenagers’ health and preventing the use of nicotine in any form by minors. We are committed to restricting underage access to our products and limiting underage appeal of our products through the 'Lighthouse Guardian Program'. This program aims to protect minors by combating underage use of e-cigarettes through concerted efforts that stretch from our product development and marketing to sales.

Stoptober Campaign

ELFBAR actively participates in smoking cessation activities and fully supports government and institutional policies. Stoptober is a smoking cessation campaign led by Public Health England that encourages smokers to start by abstaining from smoking for 28 days during the month of October. To respond to the call of the campaign, we teamed up with 20 retail outlets across the UK to launch a challenge among smokers to give up the equivalent of 50,000 cigarettes during Stoptober month, helping hardcore adult smokers quit their habits.

Take Action

Anti-counterfeiting Actions

The fight by ELFBAR against unauthorized and counterfeit devices is a long-term commitment to quality and service. As a responsible brand, we take it upon ourselves to fight against counterfeit factories worldwide and provide our customers with the best quality products and we remain zero tolerance for fake products.

Learn More

  • ELFBAR adopts advanced anti-counterfeiting traceability technology and each product is produced with a unique anti-counterfeiting security code for consumers to verify the authenticity through the ELFBAR official website.

  • ELFBAR has worked with the Chinese authorities to help shut down more than 120 counterfeit production and sales facilities including factories, warehouses and logistics operations. This has led to the seizure of more than two million finished counterfeit products, millions of packaging boxes, anti-counterfeit codes, semi-finished vaping pipes and other accessories.
